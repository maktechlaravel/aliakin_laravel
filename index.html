Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our foundation. Learn how Aroo VPN protects your online activity and personal data.

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Aroo VPN ("Aroo VPN", "we", "our", or "the Service") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using our application.

Our service is designed with privacy in mind. We aim to collect as little information as possible to operate the app reliably and securely.

1. No-Logs Policy

Aroo VPN operates under a strict no-logs policy. We do not collect, store, or track:

  • Browsing activity
  • Traffic content or destinations
  • DNS queries
  • Connection timestamps
  • Session duration
  • Bandwidth usage per user
  • IP addresses linked to user identity

Because this data is not stored, it cannot be shared, sold, or provided to third parties.

2. Personal Information

Aroo VPN does not require users to create an account or provide personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

You can use the service without providing personally identifiable information.

3. Limited Technical Information

To maintain stability and improve performance, the application may process minimal technical information such as:

  • App version
  • Crash reports (if enabled by your device or operating system)
  • General diagnostic data necessary to maintain service reliability

This information:

  • Is used only for technical purposes
  • Is not used to identify users
  • Is not used for tracking or advertising

4. Subscriptions and Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed securely by:

  • Google Play Store
  • Apple App Store

Aroo VPN does not store or process payment card details. Billing information is handled entirely by the platform providers.

5. Third-Party Services

Aroo VPN does not use:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Behavioral tracking tools
  • Profiling or fingerprinting services

Infrastructure providers may process encrypted network traffic solely to deliver the VPN service. They do not have access to user activity or identifiable personal data.

6. Security

We use modern encryption and industry-standard security practices to protect VPN connections.

We do not:

  • Modify user traffic
  • Inject advertisements
  • Monitor or inspect browsing activity

Our systems are designed to minimize data handling and reduce privacy risks.

7. Legal Requests

Because Aroo VPN does not retain activity logs or personal data, we are generally unable to associate network activity with individual users.

Any legal requests are handled in accordance with applicable laws, but the absence of stored activity data limits what information can be provided.

8. Children’s Privacy

Aroo VPN is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
