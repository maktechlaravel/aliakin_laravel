Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Learn how Aroo VPN protects your online activity and personal data.

Introduction

Aroo VPN (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to safeguarding your online privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our VPN service and mobile/desktop applications.

By using Aroo VPN, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

No-Logs Policy

At Aroo VPN, we operate under a strict No-Logs Policy. This means:

  • We do not store or log your browsing activity, connection timestamps, session duration, bandwidth usage, or IP addresses.
  • We cannot associate any specific network activity or behavior with any user.
  • Your privacy and anonymity online are fully preserved.

Information We Collect

To maintain and improve our services, we may collect limited and non-identifiable information:

  • App Diagnostics: Crash reports, app version, and performance metrics (only if you opt in).
  • Basic Account Data: Email address (if you create an account or contact support).
  • Payment Information: Managed securely by third-party payment processors — we never store your full payment details.

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  1. Operate and maintain Aroo VPN services
  2. Improve performance and troubleshoot issues
  3. Provide customer support and communicate updates
  4. Prevent abuse or security threats to the service

Security Commitment: All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard protocols (including AES-256 and WireGuard). We employ strong security measures to prevent unauthorized access or data exposure.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to support functionality (e.g., analytics, payment processing, or DNS). These providers are carefully vetted and bound by confidentiality agreements.

None of these third parties are allowed to access, collect, or log your VPN browsing data.

Data Retention

We retain minimal data strictly necessary for account management and service performance. You may request deletion of your account data at any time by contacting our support team.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for optional data collection (e.g., crash reports)
  • Request portability of your account data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@aroovpn.com.
